Task 1 & 2: Evaluating Resources

For this task, I evaluated the website iLovepdf based on professional writing characteristics. This website discusses 13 effective study methods and how to use them.

Accuracy Overall, the information on the website seems accurate and includes further links to additional resources. Parallelism There is evidence of Parallelism across the website. E.g "If you can’t effectively explain the topic in simple terms, then you haven’t yet understood it in enough detail yet." - ilovepdf.com
Active Voice This website is written in a Passive Voice. As a user, this makes the website seem very informative, however it doesn't grab my attention or feel that it is catered directly to me. Positive Voice The content is presented in a positive voice.
Biases This website presents balanced information as it explains a variety of study methods. However, I do think that a bias within the website would be its promotion of their PDF function. Strong Nouns and Verbs Use of Strong Nouns and verbs is evident throughout the website. It clearly targets the audience. E.g "A list of the best study methods, including how-to guides and online tools that will help you build successful study habits." - ilovepdf.com
Clarity The information is presented clearly and is easy to understand with headings and subheadings. It also includes links to further information if needed. Sentence Variety There is clear variation in both beginning and length of sentences.
Conciseness Although the majority of the content itself is concise, the layout of the webpage makes it look very wordy, making readers lose patience. Simple Words Use of simple words and vocabulary is consistent throughout the website making the information accessible.
Conversational & Smoothly Flowing Prose Contractions are present across the website. The vocabulary is simple and easy to understand. Not too complex. However, not very conversational. Shorter Paragraphs Information is broken into short paragraphs, however the layout of the webpage can still make the content seem overwhelming.
Correctness The content doesn’t seem to have any grammar or spelling errors. Tone and Voice The tone and voice are appropriate for this website as it targets students in High School and up

Task 2: Create Samples of Text

Sample 1

Hey you! Are feeling stressed? Do you find your study sessions unproductive? Well this is the website for you! StudySesh aims to provide all the tools to enhance your study sessions all in one place! Think of it as your very own study tool box that provides top tips on how to study efficiently as well as how to take a break. Yes TAKE A BREAK! Having a efficient and effective study regime not only eliminates the stress and anxiety that comes with approaching deadlines, but allows you to take the break you so definitely deserve! So begin your journey to an efficient StudySesh here!

Sample 2

Welcome to Study Sesh, your ultimate guide to smarter studying! Whether you're struggling to stay focused or simply want to improve your study sessions, we're here to help! StudySesh offers you a wide range of tried and true study methods to help you study more efficiently. It will include different techniques and activities you can use to customize your own study sesh, such as audios to listen to, videos to play, along with links to professional advice. Having an efficient session is not just reliant on the studying, but also having good breaks. Therefore the website also includes a page dedicated to Relaxation and Destress, to teach you ways to take your mind off work while you rest! Dive in now to discover your solution to a successful and rewarding StudySesh!

Analysis

Overall, both samples convey the general message: Introducing StudySesh as a platform that aims to guide and assist university students in studying efficiently by providing a wide range of study methods, tools and resources to enhance learning.

Sample 1 takes the more conversational approach of directly addressing the audience with questions. "Are you feeling stressed?" and "Do you find your study sessions unproductive?" are questions that creates a connection with the audience by establishing the problem that StudySesh seeks to solve. After the reader is hooked, the rest of the paragraph presents StudySesh as a study space providing all the advice and tools to answer these questions.

Sample 2 on the other hand, is more informative and detailed. It refers to the website as "your ultimate guide to smarter studying!" which introduces the solution before stating the problem.

Compared to sample 1, sample 2 goes more into detail about what the website offers, which addresses the needs of different audience profiles. Sample 1 however, uses simpler language and is more straightforward making it easier to understand. Through discussion with a member of the target audience, Sample 1 immediately grabs the attention of the reader and therefore should be the first thing the audience reads about the website. The information in sample 2 would be good as a secondary statement, proving more detail of what the site has to offer.
